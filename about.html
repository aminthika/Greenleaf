About Us

The Greenleaf Intiative, Sri Lanka, is a team of youth environmentalists, and at present we are dedicated to creating online content to promote green living and raise awareness on issues like global warming and pollution. We also hope to host tree-planting events, fundraisers, beach cleanups and other projects soon. Despite being based in Sri Lanka, our online projects are open to youth from all over the world, and anyone is welcome to become a member of the team and contribute to our cause.

For more information and quetions, contact us at greenleaf.initiativesl@gmail.com.

Our Mission and Vision

Mission: to protect and conserve natural resources and wildlife, and raise awareness about environmental issues.
Vision: to repair the destruction caused by humans to our environment and empower others to do so as well.

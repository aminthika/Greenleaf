Get Involved

There are many ways you can contribute, from helping out with our public projects to becoming a full-on member of the team. By signing up as a member you get to have your creative work featured on this website, have a say in planning our activities and above all, be a part of all our campaigns in the name of Nature.

To find out more before signing up, send your questions to greenleaf.initiativesl@gmail.com.

Other ways you can help are subscribing to our YouTube channel , where we post awareness videos and tutorials, or videos or taking part in our poster contest for the year.

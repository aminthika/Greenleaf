Mother Nature's Rights

Shine Schokman

Mother nature's rights
Are also my rights!
It is my fight!
Let's gather our might to win this fight!

Is this the truth or is this our expectation?

Nature stands for nature's side,
Man stands for man's side,
Maybe you can say that this is world war 3!
Do you see?

Nature stands for nature's side,
Man stands for man's side,
Maybe you can say that this is world war 3!
Do you see?

No? Well look,
What's all around you? Plastic, wood?
I guess the answer would be yes!
Isn't this a mess?

Man strikes
Nature strikes,
This is a never ending war!
Saving mother nature should be a law!

We shouldn't brawl!
Or stand tall!
Instead we should stand together,
Forever and ever!

Reuse
Reduce
Recycle
Should be our motto!

So let's gather together!
Protect mother nature forever!
One step at a time,
Could be just enough to stop this crime!

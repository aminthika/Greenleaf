Help Save the Trees

intoducing a radical idea by Ihansa Sahabandu

A tree is a tall perennial plant with an elongated stem, or trunk, supporting branches and leaves in most species. Trees are very important for the existence of human beings. Providing oxygen, reducing the amount of storm water runoff and protection are a few examples.

Though trees provide us with many requirements we human beings destroy them. Trees are cut and burned down for a number of reasons. Forests are logged to supply timber for wood and paper products, and to clear land for crops, cattle, and housing. It can also be eforested by mining and oil exploitation.

Yes, it is true that we also have to cut trees for our needs. One thing we can do to minimize it is, planting trees and recycling.

There is one more thing we can do to minimize cutting trees. We know that more than a thousand people die in one day and can you just imagine how many trees are being cut to make coffins in one day? It may be more than 100 trees. Why can’t we make coffins out of recycable cardboard? One thing is, they can easily be burned. We all know that a dead body won't feel anything. We cut many trees for something which is useless. Isn’t my idea useful to minimize cutting trees?

As I said we have many ways to minimize cutting trees. Come on, let’s all get together and save trees and save the earth.


See the World

by Tanushri Jayasuriya

See the world through the eyes of a bird.
And know how it feels to be free
See the world through the eyes of a lion,
Who'd spend the rest of his days in captivity

See the world through the eyes of mountain,
Who'd watch over the lands below
Standing tall over acres of wilderness
With its peaks covered in snow

See the world through the eyes of the ancient tree,
Who had sprouted a century ago
Who was once a life-giving mother
But is now just a dull wooden floor

See the world through the eyes of the people
Who were shunned by the colour of their skin
And their faith and their race and their culture,
Who were treated like mere possession or thing

See the world from a different point of view
To learn things you never would know
Every living being is important
And they all deserve to grow.

Greenleaflet 2021

Greenleaflet 2021 is a competition organized by the Greenleaf Initiative to design a poster in order to spread awareness about issues like global warming, and inform people of what they can do. Points will be awarded for creativity, originality and accuracy, and the winning entries will be featured on this website.


Eligibility

1) Contestants should be under 18 years, as of the closing date of the contest.
2) Each contestant may submit only one entry.
3) Submissions must be sent in before the 12th of September, 2021.

Process

1) Create a poster related to environmental conservation, using digital media or paper.
2) Save the file as a .JPG or .png file. For posters made on paper, place the paper on a flat surface and take a clear photograph.
3) Name the file as yourname_postertheme.JPG/png. Themes for posters are given below. (for example, aminthika_dissanayake_deforestation.png)
4) Email the submission to greenleaf.initiativesl@gmail.com. The subject or title of your email should be "Your name - poster contest submission".

Rules and Guidelines

Copyrighted, trademarked products, packaging, brand names and advertisements may not be used within the poster. All images used must be original or used with permission from its owner. In that case, proof of having obtained the creator's permission must be included in the email.

Images in which the resolution is too low to clearly see elements of the poster will be rejected. All images must be clear and with no obstructive shadows or objects.

Submissions that are received after the closing date of September 12th, 2021 will not be accepted.

By submitting your poster to the competition, you are giving the Greenleaf Initiative the right to use your poster on awareness campaigns. For privacy reasons we will not disclose the name of the contestant when doing so. However, if you wish to have your name on the poster, please inform us in your email.

Themes

1) Deforestation
3) Carbon footprint
3) Scarcity of water
4) Animal cruelty and extinction

Judging the competition

The names and qualifications of the judges will be posted on this website soon.

The factors considered when judging are as follows:

  • Originality and creativity

  • Relevance to the topic

  • Ability to raise concern and enthusiasm about the environment

Winners will be announced on this website on the 21st of September, 2021. The winning participants will be notified via email, and their posters will be featured on this website.

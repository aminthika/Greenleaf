Join Us

Become a Part of the Team

All you have to do is fill in the form, and you are officially a part of the Greenleaf Initiative. By becoming a member you get to contribute ideas for projects, take on leadership at future events, have your creative work featured on this website, meet like-minded people, and do your best in your own way to protect the planet.

Sign up

To find out more before you register, send your questions to greenleaf.initiativesl@gmail.com.

If you want to help out without making a full time commitment, you can subscribe to our YouTube channel, where we post awareness videos and tutorials, or take part in the Greenleaf poster contest for the year.
